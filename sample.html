Explore Nilambur
The land of Teak and Tranquility

Nilambur

Nilambur, located in the Malappuram district of Kerala, India, is a picturesque town known for its lush greenery, teak plantations, and rich cultural heritage. It is often referred to as the "Teak Town" due to its extensive teak forests, which are among the largest in the world. Nilambur offers a serene escape for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike. One of the main attractions in Nilambur is the Nilambur Teak Museum, which showcases the history and significance of teak wood in the region. Visitors can learn about the various uses of teak and its importance in shipbuilding and furniture making. For those interested in nature and wildlife, the nearby Nilambur Forests provide opportunities for trekking, bird watching, and exploring the diverse flora and fauna of the Western Ghats. The forests are home to several endangered species, making it a haven for wildlife enthusiasts Nilambur is also known for its cultural festivals and traditional art forms. The town hosts various events throughout the year, showcasing the vibrant culture and heritage of Kerala Overall, Nilambur is a charming destination that offers a blend of natural beauty, cultural richness, and adventure, making it a must-visit for travelers exploring Kerala.
